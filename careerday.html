international career day

December 13, 2016
University of Texas at Arlington
300 W First St, Arlington, TX 76010

This yearly event is designed to give JWAC students a wide perspective on the global landscape including international careers, what they entail and how to pursue their desired career path.

Register Now

Participating high schools students hear from selected representatives and experts on topics such as:

  • International professions
  • College degrees
  • Study abroad opportunities
  • The local impact of global trends
  • Cultural “do’s and taboos”
  • International competitiveness

All students hear about international protocol, teaching abroad, internationally focused degrees, media, and internships. From there, students choose to attend five (5) out of ten (10) break-out sessions featuring international professionals.

Featured speakers will present on global industries, including:

  • Business
  • Diplomacy
  • Engineering
  • Humanitarian service
  • Intelligence
  • Law
  • Media
  • Military Careers
  • Oil and Gas
  • Trade

In addition, several organizations have information booths at the event to provide interested students with more specific advice. Member schools may register up to 30 students. Please bring sack lunch. Contact Amy Miller, if in need of transportation assistance.
