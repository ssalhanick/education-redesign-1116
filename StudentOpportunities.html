Internship Opportunities

JWAC students and other young Council members are eligible to apply for an internship at the World Affairs Council during the fall, spring and summer sessions. Applicants must be able to commit to at least 15 hours per week in our downtown Dallas office. To apply, complete the application on our website.

Africa Boards

The Council is recruiting bright and ambitious students to help construct tri-fold boards to represent each country in Africa. The boards will be used in an educational program designed for North Texas middle school students. JWAC students who create a board according to program guidelines will receive credit for 10 hours of community service from the World Affairs Council. Hours are limited to one (1) student per board. For more information about this opportunity, email Amy Miller.
Deadline for submission (completed Africa board): 5:30 p.m. (CST), Friday, December 2, 2017.

JWAC Video Contest

Teams of up to two (2) high school students may compete in the JWAC Video Contest. The prize? An internship in the Council's Marketing and Communications Department during the summer of 2017. To apply, create a 30-45 second video that addresses these questions:

  1. How has JWAC changed my world view?
  2. How has JWAC equipped me to become a better leader?

The winning video will debut at the 2017 International Educator of the Year Award Program.

Deadline for submission: 11:59 p.m.(CST) Tuesday, January 10, 2017

Application Instructions
To apply, email Amy Miller the following material in MOV format:

  1. A finished video between 30 and 45 seconds long
  2. All raw footage captured of WACDFW programs, interviews and other relevant events not used in the final video.
